## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

### The Opportunity

The AI marketing consulting market is a $14B space growing at 31.6% CAGR, projected to reach $72.8B by 2030. Every major consulting firm — McKinsey, BCG, Accenture, Deloitte — has launched agentic marketing offerings. But they're all selling $500K+ enterprise engagements.

At the boutique level, the market is fragmented and undifferentiated:
- Fractional CMOs who added "AI" to their title
- Educators who teach AI concepts but don't implement
- Agencies who bolted AI buzzwords onto existing services
- Tool-focused consultants helping teams "adopt ChatGPT"

**Nobody is selling the foundational infrastructure that makes AI-native marketing actually work.**

This is not a positioning gap. It's an empty category.

### The Strategnik Thesis

Most companies think they have an AI strategy because their team has chatbot access. They don't have a strategy — they have a typing upgrade.

The real transformation requires building an **Intelligence Layer** — the encoded brand context, data architecture, content infrastructure, and decision systems that allow AI agents to operate with the precision, style, and strategic intent of your best marketer. Without this layer, agents are just fast interns. With it, they're a precision instrument.

Strategnik builds this layer. That's the category. That's the offering.

### What Makes This Defensible

1. **GTM Strategy** — 20+ years of B2B marketing, positioning, ICP development, pipeline mechanics
2. **Technical Infrastructure** — Data architecture, CRM design, API integrations, platform orchestration

### Tier 1: Free AI Marketing Readiness Quiz (Lead Gen)

**What it is:** A 15-20 question self-assessment CEOs/CROs take online. Automated scoring across the 6 layers. Produces a radar chart showing where they're strong and where they're exposed.

**Why this matters:** The "AI marketing readiness assessment" search term returns almost no dedicated product pages. Nobody owns this. Building it creates a permanent inbound asset.

**Sample questions:**
- "Can your marketing team produce a campaign from brief to deployment without manual handoffs between more than 2 people?"
- "Is your brand voice documented in a format that an AI system could reference — not a PDF, but structured data?"
- "What percentage of your CRM data would you trust an autonomous system to act on without human review?"
- "When a product usage signal fires (trial activation, feature adoption, churn risk), how long before marketing responds?"

**Output:** Scored report with layer-by-layer assessment + clear CTA to the paid diagnostic.

**Build cost:** Low. Can be built with Typeform/Tally + a scoring logic layer. Or a custom page on strategnik.com.

### Tier 2: The Intelligence Layer Diagnostic ($10K-$15K)

**Duration:** 2 weeks

**Inputs:**
- 3-4 team interviews (2 hrs total)
- Current tech stack inventory
- Current org chart + open roles / planned hires
- 90-day marketing calendar
- CRM/CDP access for data quality audit
- Brand guidelines and content assets

**Deliverable — three sections:**

1. **Layer-by-Layer Gap Analysis** — Each of the 6 layers scored (0-5 maturity), with specific findings. Where is the context layer that agents need? What exists? What's missing? What's broken?

2. **The Execution Tax** — Quantified: hours spent on manual work, cost per output, signal-to-response latency, content production cycle time. The "here's what you're burning" section.

3. **The Orchestrated Model** — What it looks like rebuilt: team roles, agent architecture, data flows, decision rights matrix, content system design. Plus a 90-day build roadmap sequenced by leverage.

**Why $10-15K:** Below the cost of one month of a marketing hire. CEO can approve without board involvement. High enough to signal expertise. Naturally pulls through to architecture/build engagement.

### Tier 3: Architecture ($40K-$75K)

**Duration:** 4-8 weeks

**What gets built:**
- Detailed data architecture specifications (CRM properties, signal definitions, scoring models, workflow logic)
- Brand context documentation in machine-readable format (not a PDF — structured reference that agents consume)
- Content infrastructure design (templates, schemas, production workflows, editorial standards)
- Distribution orchestration blueprint (platform-by-platform signal routing, trigger conditions, escalation paths)
- Decision rights matrix (what's autonomous, what's human-approved, what escalates)
- Measurement framework (what to measure, how to measure, feedback loop design)
- Team redesign recommendations (role definitions, skill requirements, hiring/transition plan)

### Tier 4: Embedded Build ($25-40K/month, 3-6 months)

**What happens:**
- Strategnik builds and deploys the Intelligence Layer across 1-3 marketing workstreams
- Stand up actual agent workflows on real campaigns with guardrails
- Train the team to operate as system designers and experience governors, not campaign operators
- SOPs, governance playbook, operating procedures
- Transition to self-sufficient operation

**This is where your Afiniti/Codiac work is the proof point.** You've done this. You've built ICP frameworks, 1,040-account ABM architectures, product-signal-driven nurture systems, editorial production kits, JSON-LD schemas, multi-platform orchestration. The Embedded Build is the productized version of what you've already delivered.
