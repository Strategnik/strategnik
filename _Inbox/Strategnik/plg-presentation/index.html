The Problem

When Frictionless Breaks

The PLG orthodoxy says remove all friction. But when your API is too easy to hit, you get usage data that looks great and predicts nothing.

The signal-to-noise ratio had collapsed, and nobody could tell the difference.
01

What Happens When Signal Collapses

Sales wastes cycles on phantom intent

PQLs stop predicting conversion. Sales stops trusting the system. Real prospects get buried.

Usage metrics become vanity metrics

MAU goes up. Revenue doesn't. The metric becomes self-reinforcing and self-deceiving.

ICP definition blurs

Product decisions get made on aggregate behavior of a user base that's 80% non-buyers.

02

The Thesis

Friction as Signal Architecture

Make it easy to use your product for the right reasons, and slightly less easy for the wrong ones.

A twelve-field form and a work email requirement are both “friction.” One is process friction imposed on the buyer. The other is signal friction that benefits everyone.

The Formula

PQL Score = (Production × 0.4) + (Enterprise × 0.4) + (Engagement × 0.2)
IF score > 70 AND team_size ≥ 3  →  Route to Sales

Start simple. Advanced variants split developer-led vs. top-down.

11

Routing Logic

Two Motions

Developer-Led

High production usage + low enterprise signals

Route to DevRel.

Nurture with technical content. Sales calls kill this motion.

Top-Down

Enterprise signals + moderate usage

Route to Enterprise AE.

Speed matters. Exec evaluations move fast or die.

16

Watch For

Four Failure Modes

×
Loose friction + aggressive PQL thresholds

→ Phantom pipeline. Tighten signup, add enterprise signals.

×
Tight friction + weak brand infrastructure

→ Growth ceiling. Build the content engine.

×
Strong usage signals + no conversion wall

→ Permanently free users. Recalibrate limits and feature gates.

×
B2B marketing tactics aimed at developers

→ Credibility death. GitHub first. Marketing second.

17

The Framework in One Sentence

The orthodoxy says remove all friction. The physics say apply friction where it does useful work, and remove it everywhere else.
